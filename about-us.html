ABOUT US

LAMISCHOLERO NIGERIA LIMITED is a limited liability company incorporated with the aim of being a recognized reputable Building, Engineering And Design Company.
‍
The Company with RC No. 1256695 was incorporated on the 23th of April, 2015 at the Corporate Affairs Commission, Abuja under the Companies and Allied Matters Act, 1990.Our Corporate Mission is to make Projects delivery friendly and value for quality services with Innovative Construction Products.

Our areas of specialties are:·


-Estate and Residential Development·      
-Factory/Warehouse Development
-Engineering Design/Interior Decoration and Finishes·        
-Procurement of Goods and Services·        
-Light weight Concrete·      
-Water Proof Plastering
-Soil/Road Stabilization
-Drainage and Culverts
-Solar Inverter Installations
-Street Lighting
-External Civil Works
